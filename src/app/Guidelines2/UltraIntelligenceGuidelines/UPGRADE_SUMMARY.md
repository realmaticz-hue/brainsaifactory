## 🚀 How to Use

### **Quick Start - App Builder:**

1. Click **"App Builder"** button (purple, top-right)
2. Describe your app in detail:
   ```
   "Build a recipe sharing app with user profiles, recipe cards
   with ingredients and instructions, favorites, and comments"
   ```
3. Click **"Generate Complete App"**
4. Wait 10-20 seconds
5. Browse tabs: **Preview** → **Code** → **Database** → **API** → **Export**
6. Export to ZIP, XCode, or Android Studio
7. Deploy!

### **Quick Start - Avatar Generator:**

1. Click **"3D Avatar Generator"** button (green, top-right)
2. Choose mode:
   - **Text**: Describe avatar
   - **Photo**: Upload face photo
   - **Preset**: Pick from 6 professionals
3. Click **"Generate Avatar"**
4. Wait 8-15 seconds
5. Customize in tabs: **Customize** → **Voice** → **Animate** → **Export**
6. Export as MP4 video or 3D model
7. Use in ads!
